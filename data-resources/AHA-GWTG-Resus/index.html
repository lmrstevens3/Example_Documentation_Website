About the GWTG® Resuscitation Registry Data

The Get With the Guidelines® Resuscitation Registry is a voluntary quality improvement registry. Information and documentation regarding the registry data, data usage are provided in the workspace as well as on this site. SOMETHING ABOUT ADULT vs. PEDIATRIC? CPA vs. MET?

This data was collected from XXX hospitals TIME?? and is comprised of 451,792 records and 317 measures. The registry entry criteria instruct hospitals to enter all patients who meet the following:

  1. Experience a cardiopulmonary resuscitation event, defined as either pulselessness or a pulse with inadequate perfusion requiring Chest compressions and/or Defibrillation of ventricular fibrillation or pulseless ventricular tachycardia.
  2. The event elicits EITHER a hospital-wide (e.g., for general inpatient area) or unit-based (ICU, ED, OR, PACU, delivery room, etc.) emergency response by acute care facility personnel.
